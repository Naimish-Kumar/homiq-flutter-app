# **Project Document: Homiq AI – Virtual Interior Designer**

---

## **📌 1\. Project Overview**

**App Name:** Homiq AI  
 **Package Name:** `com.homiq.app`  
 **Category:** Home Design / AI / Lifestyle  
 **Platform:** Android & iOS (Flutter)

### **🧠 Description:**

Homiq AI is an **AI-powered virtual interior design app** that allows users to upload room images and instantly get redesigned interiors based on selected styles, budgets, and preferences.

The app aims to make interior design:

* Affordable 💰  
* Accessible 📱  
* Instant ⚡

---

## **🎯 2\. Objectives**

* Provide **instant room design suggestions using AI**  
* Help users **visualize home transformations**  
* Offer **budget-based interior solutions**  
* Enable **furniture discovery & purchase integration**

2. Uploads room image  
3. Selects style & budget
